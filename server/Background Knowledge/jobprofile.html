Job Profile: Cloud Solutions Architect

Job Overview

The Cloud Solutions Architect is responsible for designing and implementing cloud-based solutions that meet the business needs of the organization. This role involves collaboration with development teams, IT operations, and stakeholders to ensure scalability, reliability, and efficiency of cloud services.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement cloud-based architectures and solutions.
  • Collaborate with development teams to ensure cloud compatibility.
  • Optimize cloud environments for performance and cost-efficiency.
  • Monitor and maintain cloud security protocols and compliance.
  • Provide guidance and support for cloud migration projects.

Required Skills and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Proven experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Strong understanding of networking, storage, and virtualization.
  • Experience with DevOps practices and tools (CI/CD pipelines).
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Certification in cloud technologies (AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Azure Solutions Architect Expert).
  • Experience with containerization tools (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Knowledge of scripting languages (Python, Bash).
  • Experience with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation).

Work Environment

This role is hybrid, with flexibility to work both remotely and on-site at our headquarters in [City, State]. The Cloud Solutions Architect will work closely with cross-functional teams in a collaborative and innovative environment.
